Checks for a 1-sum decomposition of a given ternary or binary linear matroid.

Initializes the *pdecomposition to a partial decomposition tree. If the matroid is a 1-sum then *pdecomposition will be a TU_DEC_ONE_SUM tree node and its children will be initialized with corresponding sequentially connected submatrices. Otherwise, *pdecomposition itself will be initialized with a sequentially connected permutation of the given matroid.
