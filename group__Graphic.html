Computes the binary representation matrix for a given graph.

Let \( G = (V,E) \) be an undirected graph with nodes \( V \) and edges \( E \). Let \( T \subseteq E \) be a spanning forest of \( G \). The binary representation matrix \( M := M(G,T) \) is a matrix \( M \in \{0,1\}^{T \times (E \setminus T)} \) with \( M_{e,f} = 1 \iff e \) belongs to the unique cycle in \( T \cup \{f\} \).

Computes \( M(G,T) \) for given \( G \) and spanning forest \( T \) given by forestEdges. If forestEdges is NULL, \( T \) is some spanning forest \( T \) of \( G \) is computed. The ordering of the columns can be specified via coforestEdges.

Note
The function computes a representation matrix of \( G \) regardless of whether forestEdges is a correct spanning tree. This is indicated via *pisCorrectForest.

Computes the ternary representation matrix for a given graph.

Let \( G = (V,E) \) be a directed graph with nodes \( V \) and edges \( E \). Let \( T \subseteq E \) be a spanning forest of \( G \), directed arbitrarily. The ternary representation matrix \( M := M(G,T) \) is a matrix \( M \in \{-1,0,1\}^{T \times (E \setminus T)} \) with \( M_{e,\{u,v\}} = +1 \) (resp. \( M_{e,\{u,v\}} \) if \( e \) is a forward edge (resp. backward edge) on the unique \( s \)- \( t \)-path in \( T \).

Computes \( M(G,T) \) for given \( G \) and spanning forest \( T \) given by forestEdges. The direction of the edges is specified by edgesReversed. If forestEdges is NULL, \( T \) is some spanning forest \( T \) of \( G \) is computed. The ordering of the columns can be specified via coforestEdges.

Note
The function computes a representation matrix of \( G \) regardless of whether forestEdges is a correct spanning tree. This is indicated via *pisCorrectForest.

Tests a binary matrix for graphicness.

Let \( G = (V,E) \) be an undirected graph with nodes \( V \) and edges \( E \). Let \( T \subseteq E \) be a spanning forest of \( G \). The binary representation matrix \( M := M(G,T) \) is a matrix \( M \in \{0,1\}^{T \times (E \setminus T)} \) with \( M_{e,f} = 1 \iff e \) belongs to the unique cycle in \( T \cup \{f\} \).

Tests if \( M = M(G,T) \) for some graph \( G \) and some spanning forest \( T \) of G and sets *pisGraphic accordingly. The matrix \( M \) is given by \( M^{\mathsf{T}} := \) transpose.

If \( M \) is such a representation matrix and pgraph != NULL, then one possible graph \( G \) is computed and stored in *pgraph. The caller must release the memory via TUgraphFree. If in addition to pgraph also pforestEdges (resp. pcoforestEdges) != NULL, then a corresponding spanning forest \( T \) (resp. its complement \( E \setminus T \) is stored in *pforestEdges (resp. pcoforestEdges). The caller must release the memory via TUfreeBlockArray.

If \( M \) is not such a representation matrix and psubmatrix != NULL, then a minimal submatrix of \( M \) with the same property is computed and stored in *psubmatrix. The caller must release the memory via TUsubmatFree.

Finds an inclusion-wise maximal subset of columns that induce a graphic binary submatrix.

Let \( G = (V,E) \) be an undirected graph with nodes \( V \) and edges \( E \). Let \( T \subseteq E \) be a spanning forest of \( G \). The binary representation matrix \( M := M(G,T) \) is a matrix \( M \in \{0,1\}^{T \times (E \setminus T)} \) with \( M_{e,f} = 1 \iff e \) belongs to the unique cycle in \( T \cup \{f\} \).

Finds an inclusion-wise maximal subset \( J \) of columns of \( M \) such that \( M_{\star,J} \) is a binary representation matrix. To achieve this, it tries to append columns in the order given by orderedColumns, maintaining graphicness.
